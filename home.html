Seamless Bursary System

Hon. Tom Ndaka will introduce a streamlined bursary application process to ensure no student is left behind. Key features include:

  • Online application portal for convenience.
  • Real-time tracking of application status.
  • Fair and need-based allocation of funds.
  • Transparent disbursement process.
