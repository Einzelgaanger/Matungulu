Hon. Tom Ndaka's Bursary Management System Manifesto

A transparent and efficient platform to manage CDF bursaries for the students of Matungulu. Ensuring every deserving student gets the support they need to achieve their dreams.

Key Features of the Bursary System

Online Application Portal

Students and parents can easily apply for bursaries online, eliminating the need for tedious paperwork and long queues.

Transparent Approval Process

Applications are reviewed fairly and transparently, with real-time updates on the status of each application.

Efficient Fund Disbursement

Funds are disbursed directly to schools or students' accounts, ensuring timely and accurate delivery of support.

How the Bursary System Works

Streamlined Process for Students and Parents

Hon. Tom Ndaka's Bursary Management System is designed to make the application process simple, transparent, and efficient. Here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Create an account on the portal.
  • Step 2: Fill out the bursary application form online.
  • Step 3: Upload required documents for verification.
  • Step 4: Track your application status in real-time.
  • Step 5: Receive notifications upon approval and disbursement.
